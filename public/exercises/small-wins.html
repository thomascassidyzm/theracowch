Small Wins Celebration

Acknowledge one small win from today

It doesn't have to be huge — just something you did that moved you forward. Recognising small wins trains your brain to notice progress instead of only seeing what's left to do.

How it works

1 Pause: Stop what you're doing
2 Reflect: What's one small thing you did today?
3 Acknowledge: Write it down below
4 Feel: Notice how it feels to recognize this
5 Lock it in: Take a breath and let it sink in
Win celebrated! You're making progress. 🎉
