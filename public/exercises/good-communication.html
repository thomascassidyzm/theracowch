Tips for Delivery:
  • Pick the right time - not during an argument or when rushed
  • Use "I" statements, not "You" accusations
  • Stay specific - don't bring up old issues
  • Listen to their response without interrupting
  • Be prepared to compromise, but hold your boundary
  • Avoid using "You always..." or "You never..." as this encourages defensiveness in the other person
