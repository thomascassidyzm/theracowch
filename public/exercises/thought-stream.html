Thought Stream

In Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, we practice observing our thoughts without judgment.

"Imagine you're sitting beside a gentle stream. Each thought that comes to mind is placed on a leaf and allowed to float away."

You don't need to fight your thoughts or make them disappear. Simply acknowledge them, place them on a leaf, and watch them drift downstream.

This practice helps create distance between you and your thoughts, reminding you that you are not your thoughts.
