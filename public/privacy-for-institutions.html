Cowch Student data — a one-page summary

How Cowch handles student data

A short summary for wellbeing and data-protection teams. The headline: there is essentially no student data for the institution to process.

Endorsing, linking to, or sharing Cowch does not create a data-processing relationship for your institution. Students use Cowch directly; their journals and chat history live on their own device; we hold no central student database and require no account to use it. So there is no student personal data flowing through you to assess.

Where data lives

On the student's deviceJournal entries, exercise inputs, chat history, preferences and progress — stored in their own browser. They stay on that device unless the student exports them.
In transit, briefly → AI provider (Anthropic, US)When a student messages Mandy, that message + recent context is sent over TLS purely to generate a reply. Not stored long-term by us; not used to train AI models (per provider contract).
Hosting / CDN (Vercel)Serves the app and passes requests through. Short-term operational logs only.
Aggregate analytics (cookieless)Anonymous, aggregate usage only (e.g. visits, installs). No cookies, no cross-site tracking, no personal data, and never message or journal content.

What Cowch does not do

  • No central database of student identities or content.
  • No account, login, name or email required for a student to use it.
  • No selling of data, no advertising, no AI training on user content.
  • No tracking of students across other websites.

Safeguarding & clinical safety — set expectations clearly

Cowch is a non-clinical wellbeing tool. It does not diagnose, assess, triage, or monitor risk, and it is not a crisis or safety system. Its “Need help now?” button signposts students to national crisis lines (and can be pointed at your own services). Because it is non-clinical and is not procured by the institution as a health-IT system, it does not bring the institution into clinical-safety standards such as DTAC or DCB0129. It is for adults (18+) and is not a replacement for therapy or medical care.
