About Mandy & Cowch

A little more about who's behind this app, how it works, and how it’s meant to help you.

A reminder: Cowch is a wellbeing-support app. It is not a replacement for clinical therapy, medical care, or emergency services. If you’re in crisis, please contact 999 (UK), 911 (US), NHS 111 option 2, Samaritans 116 123, or text SHOUT to 85258.

Who is Mandy?

Mandy Kloppers is a qualified Cognitive Behavioural Therapist with over two decades of experience helping people navigate anxiety, depression, relationships and personal growth. Her credentials are:

  • BA (UNISA)
  • PG Dip Psychology (Open)
  • PG Dip CBT (New Buckinghamshire)
  • BABCP-accredited CBT therapist

For her one-to-one therapy work, visit thoughtsonlifeandlove.com.

What is Cowch?

Cowch is a warm, private wellbeing companion. Inside the app you’ll find:

  • Mandy — an AI reflection partner trained on CBT, ACT and compassion-focused approaches, with Mandy Kloppers’ therapeutic voice.
  • The IMAGINE framework — seven domains for looking after your mind, body and relationships.
  • Tiny daily exercises — box breathing, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, gratitude, tiny wins, and more.
  • A private, on-device journal — your entries stay on your own device.
  • “Need help now?” — one-tap routing to crisis lines when things feel too much.

The IMAGINE framework

Seven small letters, seven big areas of wellbeing. Each one gets its own page in the app with exercises you can try:

I — I, Me, MyselfKnow yourself deeply.
M — MindFind your calm.
A — AcceptEmbrace what is.
G — GratitudeNotice the good.
I — InteractionsShow up in relationships.
N — NurturingPlay, rest, recharge.
E — ExploringTry something new.

How Mandy “sounds”

Mandy is calm, grounded, practical and gently challenging when needed. She favours clarity and usefulness over softness for its own sake. She won’t label you, won’t rush to fix you, and won’t pretend she’s human. If she notices a pattern she’ll check it with you first, and if she doesn’t have enough evidence she’ll say so.

How Cowch handles privacy

Your chats and journal entries live on your device. Messages you send Mandy are sent to our AI model provider solely to generate a reply. We don’t sell data, advertise against it, or use it to train AI. See the Privacy Policy for the full detail.
