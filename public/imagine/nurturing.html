Inner Child Work

Explore these prompts to reconnect with your playful, creative inner child. No pressure, no judgment.

  • What was your favorite childhood game or activity? ✓
  • What would 8-year-old you want to do this weekend? ✓
  • What silly thing makes you laugh every time? ✓
  • When was the last time you played just for fun? ✓
  • What creative activity did you love as a child? ✓
  • What would you do if you had a whole day with no responsibilities? ✓
  • What's something playful you've been wanting to try? ✓
  • How do you feel when you allow yourself to be playful? ✓
  • What permission does your inner child need from you? ✓
  • What small act of play can you do today? ✓
