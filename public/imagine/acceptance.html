Acceptance

Embracing Your Current State

Acceptance doesn't mean approval or giving up. It means letting go of the extra suffering that comes from resisting reality. When we accept what is, we free ourselves to move forward with clarity and peace.

Radical Acceptance

Sometimes we hold on to situations we can't change. Radical acceptance helps you let go of extra suffering by acknowledging what is.

HEAL Framework

Challenge negative thoughts with self-compassion. Is the thought Helpful? Where's the Evidence? What's an Alternative? What would you say to a Loved one?
3

Let the Feeling Rise & Fall

Emotions come in waves, not permanent states. This exercise helps you ride a difficult feeling instead of fighting it — watching it peak and naturally subside.
