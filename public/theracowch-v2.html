Body Scan - Progressive Muscle Relaxation ğŸ§˜

Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR) helps you become aware of tension and learn to relax.

When we're tense, we involuntarily send a message to our brains that we feel in danger. PMR helps us feel calm and turn off the amygdala.

Instructions:

  1. Sit or lie down comfortably
  2. Start with your feet: tense the muscles for 5 seconds, then release and notice the relaxation
  3. Move upward through: legs, stomach, hands, arms, shoulders, face
  4. Tense each group for 5 seconds, then relax
  5. Breathe slowly and notice the difference between tension and calm

ğŸ’¡ Tip: Perfect for better sleep - try before bedtime!

Radical Acceptance ğŸ•Šï¸

Acceptance Reaffirmation Exercise:

  1. Take a slow breath and tell yourself: "This is the reality right now."
  2. Whisper: "I don't have to fight this. I can let go."
  3. Remind yourself: "Acceptance helps me move forward."
  4. Place your hand on your heart and notice the warmth.
  5. Let the sense of relief sink in.
