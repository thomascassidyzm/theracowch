The IMAGINE Framework

IMAGINE is a holistic therapeutic framework with 7 interconnected domains for mental wellbeing and personal growth.

I Introspection & Self-Awareness

Understanding your inner world, patterns, and self-concept. Who are you beyond external labels?

M Mindfulness

Being present in the moment. Observing thoughts and feelings without judgment.

A Acceptance

Letting go of control. Making peace with what is, while working toward what could be.

G Gratitude

Recognizing the good. Shifting focus from what's missing to what's present.

I Interactions & Relationships

Nurturing connections. Setting boundaries. Understanding relationship patterns.

N Nurturing & Self-Care

Treating yourself with kindness. Rest, play, and activities that restore you.

E Exploring & Understanding Patterns

Examining recurring themes in your life. Understanding your story to rewrite it.

Chat with Mandy to explore any of these domains. Just say what's on your mind, and we'll explore together.

Exercise Library

These therapeutic exercises can help you practice the skills we talk about. Ask Mandy to guide you through any of these:

Grounding & Calming

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Box Breathing: Breathe in 4 counts, hold 4, out 4, hold 4
  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Breathe in 4 counts, hold 7, out 8
  • Body Scan: Progressive muscle relaxation from head to toe

Cognitive Work

  • Thought Unhooking: Notice thoughts without getting caught in them
  • Thought Defusion: Say "I'm having the thought that..." to create distance
  • Evidence For/Against: Examine beliefs objectively

Self-Compassion

  • H-E-A-L Method: Here, Empathize, Accept, Love yourself through difficulty
  • Self-Compassion Break: "This is hard. Everyone struggles. May I be kind to myself."
  • Compassionate Letter: Write to yourself as you would a dear friend

Values & Purpose

  • Values Compass: Identify what truly matters to you
  • Valued Living Assessment: How aligned are your actions with your values?

Acceptance

  • Radical Acceptance: Making peace with reality as it is
  • Leaves on a Stream: Watch thoughts float by like leaves on water

Just ask Mandy: "Can you guide me through [exercise name]?" and we'll do it together.
